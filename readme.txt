Elegantly displays your staff members.

== Description ==

This is the amazing PJS standard staff manager built by the PlainJoe Studios team located in Southern California.

At a glance, this plugin does the following:

* Displays your staff members elegantly to website visitors.
* Includes tags for campuses and teams for each staff member.
